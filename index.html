Our Story

The band was formed in 2015, when the founding members from a same youth organization gathered and expressed interest to play original music outside their ministry. The band is composed of Jalo Aningat on vocals and keyboards, Nikko Gonzaga on rhythm guitars, JM Albelda on lead guitars, Ivan Havana on bass, and Mark 'Spex' Baba on drums. Currently, Ivan is on hiatus, so Elijah Balingcongan replaces him as a session bassist.
